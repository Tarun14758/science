About PCM: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics

The PCM stream is ideal for students who are inclined towards technical and analytical careers. This combination provides a strong foundation for engineering, technology, and scientific research.

Why Choose PCM?

Choosing PCM opens up a vast number of career paths in both traditional and emerging fields such as robotics, artificial intelligence, and space science. It develops critical thinking and problem-solving abilities.

Subjects in PCM Stream

The PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics) stream in 11th and 12th grade typically involves 5 main subjects, thoughtfully structured to develop analytical thinking and prepare students for competitive careers in science and engineering.
