About PCMB: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology

PCMB combines the analytical powers of mathematics with the biological sciences. It is ideal for students who are still exploring both engineering and medical career paths and want to keep both options open.

Why Choose PCMB?

It gives flexibility to pursue careers in both engineering and medical fields. It develops logical thinking, problem-solving, and scientific research skills.
