About PCB: Physics, Chemistry, Biology

The PCB stream is ideal for students who are interested in life sciences and health-related fields. This combination is perfect for those aspiring to become doctors, researchers, or professionals in the biological sciences.

Why Choose PCB?

Choosing PCB offers a gateway to diverse fields like medicine, biotechnology, pharmacy, and environmental science. It develops observational, analytical, and research-based thinking.

Subjects in PCB Stream

The PCB (Physics, Chemistry, Biology) stream in 11th and 12th grade usually includes 5 main subjects, designed to build a deep understanding of living systems and scientific concepts.
